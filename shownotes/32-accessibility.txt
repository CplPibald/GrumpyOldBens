ADA disabilities
 record or history of a "mental or physical impairment that prevents participation in major life activities"
   physical = neurological, sense, muscular, digestive, reproductive, hemic/lymphatic,, endocrine, skin, genitourinary
   mental = speech/hearing, visual, retardation, drug addiction, HIV, diabetes
   Cancer and Multiple sclerosis are not considered disabilities
